Waste Segregation Tips

Here are some useful tips to segregate your waste:

  • Biodegradable Waste: Includes food scraps, garden waste, and other organic matter. Always dispose of it in a compostable bag.
  • Non-biodegradable Waste: Includes plastics, metals, glass, and other materials that cannot be composted. Ensure they are cleaned and sorted for recycling.
  • Recyclable Materials: Items such as paper, cardboard, and plastics (check for recycling symbols) can be placed in the recycling bin.
  • Hazardous Waste: Batteries, e-waste, and chemicals should be disposed of separately in designated bins to prevent environmental damage.
